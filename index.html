About Amani Maternal Bliss Care

We are dedicated to empowering mothers during their journey through pregnancy, birth and early motherhood. Our focus is twofold: improve maternal health outcomes in communities, and help employers build family-friendly workplaces that retain and support talent.

Mission: To create healthier workplaces and happier families.

Vision: To be the leading maternal wellness partner for businesses and communities.

Values: Care • Compassion • Professionalism • Innovation.

Why choose us?

  • Comprehensive maternal support
  • Compassionate, trained team
  • Evidence-based, measurable programmes
  • Tailored corporate solutions

About the Co-Founders

Immaculate Namanda and Monica Nabakooza bring decades of clinical maternal care and community engagement experience. Together they combine clinical expertise with corporate program design to deliver maternal wellness services that are compassionate, measurable and scalable. Their leadership focuses on evidence-based care, workplace integration, and community outreach ensuring both individual mothers and organizations receive purposeful support.

Their complementary skillsclinical nursing leadership, programme design, and corporate partnerships — guide Amani’s mission to improve maternal outcomes while strengthening employee wellbeing and retention.

Our Services

Holistic maternal services designed for workplaces, homes and communities.

Nursing Aids at Work

Skilled nursing assistants to support lactating mothers during work hours, improving comfort and productivity.
